BOWLS CLUB BRIDGE SECTION
The Bridge Section of the LMBC was founded in 2021 by two members of the Bowling Club - Gill Daniels and Jenny Young. Since then it has developed into a thriving club of up to seven tables, using an electronic scoring system to enter and produce the results.
